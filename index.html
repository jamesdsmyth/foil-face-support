App Issues

If Foil Face is freezing, crashing, or behaving unexpectedly, try closing and reopening the app, then restart your device if the issue continues.

Photos & Access

If the app cannot access your photos or camera, open iOS Settings, find Foil Face, and confirm the permissions you want to use are enabled.

Feedback

Ideas, feature requests, and bug reports are welcome. The more specific the note, the easier it is to improve the app.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug?

Email support with what you expected to happen, what actually happened, and the steps that seem to trigger the issue.

Why is photo or camera access not working?

Check your device permissions in Settings. If permissions are already enabled, update Foil Face to the latest version and restart the app.

How do I request a new feature?

Send your idea by email and include how you would use it. Practical examples help prioritize what to build next.

Where can I find privacy information?

For privacy questions, contact support and include "Privacy" in the subject line so the request can be handled appropriately.

Contact Support

For the fastest help, include your Foil Face app version, iPhone or iPad model, iOS version, and a brief description of the issue.
